About Us

At Sunny Auto Service we provide a wide variety of services including computer diagnostics, tire services, mechanical repairs, electrical repairs, windshield and glass repairs, and other type of services. All at affordable pricing, our hourly rate starts at $80.00 per hour. Our service is fast and professional. We can also pick you up and drop you back to your place. We provide English, Mandarin and Cantonese services. We also recyle all used tires.

Serving you all our heart and help you and your family drive a safe and efficient vehicle is our mission.

Our prices are based on top quality parts, professional experience.

We will always be honest to recommend services or parts your vehicle need.
